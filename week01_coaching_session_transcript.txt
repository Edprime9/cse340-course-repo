Coach: Great example, Morrison Eli. This snippet shows both an EJS page and the use of EJS partials.

Coach: Can you explain why using partials might be better than copying the same header and footer HTML into every EJS page manually?

Morrison Eli:
Every time you copy-paste, there’s a chance you'll accidentally delete a closing </div> or miss a <script> tag in one of the files. This leads to "ghost bugs" where the site looks perfect on the Home page but breaks on the Categories page. Partials ensure the structure is identical everywhere.

Coach: Excellent explanation, Morrison Eli.
